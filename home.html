Backdoor

"Love can open doors...but some lead to dark places."

Overview:

When Ella, a struggling novelist, matches with Dylan, a charismatic tech entrepreneur, her world shifts. Everything seems perfect... But beneath his charm lies something darker—a hidden world of deception, stolen identities, and crimes committed behind a screen.

Whispers of hidden secrets. Shadows in his past. As she digs deeper, she uncovers something far more terrifying than heartbreak. Dylan isn’t just a man—he’s a ghost in the system, a master of digital deception, and someone who always stays one step ahead.

Now, Ella finds herself trapped in a world where reality is rewritten at the press of a key, where identities vanish, where trust is the deadliest illusion, and where the wrong move could mean losing more than just her story.

She thought she was writing a love story. She’s actually living a cybercrime thriller. And in this game, losing means more than heartbreak—it means being erased.

Some backdoors should never be opened.
